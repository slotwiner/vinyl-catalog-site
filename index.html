Vinyl Condition Grades

MMintPerfect, unplayed. Still in original shrink wrap.
NMNear MintNearly perfect. Played but shows no signs of wear. Sleeve intact, no ring wear.
VG+Very Good+Light surface marks that don't affect play. Minor sleeve wear. Most collector-grade used records fall here.
VGVery GoodNoticeable surface noise, light scratches. Sleeve has ring wear, seam splits, or writing. Still enjoyable to listen to.
G+Good+Plays through without skipping but has consistent surface noise and visible scratches.
GGoodSignificant wear and noise. May skip on some tracks. Sleeve heavily worn.
FFairPlays but with heavy distortion, skips, or damage. Sleeve damaged.
PPoorBarely playable or unplayable. Cracked, warped, or deeply scratched.
